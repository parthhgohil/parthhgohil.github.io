About Me

From the quarterdeck
to the factory floor

I am all about agency, experimentation, and exploration. My decade in the Navy was super enriching — it taught me more about life than work. It gave me comrades for life and memories I will cherish forever.

Along the way, something crystallised: money alone doesn't make you happy. The Navy's structured life gave me enough — a good house, a car, holidays, freedom from debt. But it also showed me that 10x money wouldn't mean 10x happiness. That's not an exponential curve I wanted to chase.

So I made a choice. Leave the forces. Do an MBA at IIM Ahmedabad. Figure out what I actually wanted. Two options on the table: take a job, save for 20 years, retire quietly — or take a risk, build something, and leave behind something that has my name on it as a creator.

I chose the second. That choice is called KWIK. Even if I fail, Plan A is still there. But in any case — life is beautiful when things are in motion.
